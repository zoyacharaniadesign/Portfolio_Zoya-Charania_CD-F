Process


The WHAT

My journey into graphic design began with a lifelong passion for arts and crafts, but it was in design that I found my true connection. Inspired by YouTubers who brought the field to life and encouraged by my dad, I realized that design was the perfect blend of creativity and purpose for me. Now, as a graphic designer, I’m excited to craft work that makes an impact.

The WHY

Design, for me, is about creating meaning and connection. I’ve always been driven to turn ideas into visuals that resonate and make an impact. My inspiration comes from seeing how a single image or design can evoke emotion, communicate complex ideas, and even change perspectives. I’m motivated by the possibility of influencing people’s experiences through creative storytelling.

The HOW

My design process is driven by curiosity and attention to detail. I begin with research to understand the nuances of the topic. Using Adobe Creative Cloud, I efficiently bring concepts to life with tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I focus on balancing creativity with functionality, ensuring my designs are both visually appealing and communicative. Each project is an opportunity to innovate, combining structure and flexibility to create purposeful, fresh designs.
